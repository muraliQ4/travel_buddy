# 🎯 Quick User Guide: New Features

## Request System - How It Works

### 📋 For Trip Join Requests

#### Step 1: Find a Trip
Look for trips on the home page. You'll see cards with trip details.

#### Step 2: Click the Button
You'll see one of these buttons:

| Button | Meaning | What You Can Do |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **➕ Join Trip** (Teal) | You haven't requested yet | Click to send a join request |
| **⏳ Request Pending** (Yellow) | Your request is waiting | Nothing - just wait for approval |
| **✅ Active - Open Chat** (Green) | You're accepted! | Click to open group chat |
| **🔒 Trip Full** (Gray) | No more space | Can't join this trip |

#### Important Rules:
- ✅ You can only send ONE request per trip
- ✅ Once you click "Join Trip", the button changes to "Request Pending"
- ✅ When the trip creator accepts you, it becomes "Active"
- ❌ You cannot send another request for the same trip

---

### 🚗 For Ride Share Bookings

#### Step 1: Find a Ride
Browse rideshare listings on the home page.

#### Step 2: Click the Button
You'll see one of these buttons:

| Button | Meaning | What You Can Do |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **🎫 Book Seat** (Green) | Seats available | Click to book a seat |
| **⏳ Request Pending** (Yellow) | Your booking is waiting | Wait for driver approval |
| **✅ Active - View Details** (Green) | Booking confirmed! | Click to see details |
| **🔒 Fully Booked** (Gray) | No seats left | Can't book this ride |

#### Important Rules:
- ✅ You can only send ONE booking per ride
- ✅ Once you click "Book Seat", the button changes to "Request Pending"
- ✅ When the driver accepts, it becomes "Active"
- ❌ You cannot send another booking for the same ride

## 👤 Profile Editing

### How to Edit Your Profile

1. **Open Profile Page**
   - Click the Profile icon (👤) at the bottom navigation

2. **Click Edit Button**
   - Look for the "Edit Profile" button at the top

3. **Fill in Your Information**

#### Required Fields (Must fill):
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Username
- Country
- City

#### Optional Fields (Recommended):
- **Personal Details:**
  - Date of Birth
  - Gender
  - Nationality

- **Location:**
  - Full Address
  - Languages you speak (click to select multiple)

- **About Me:**
  - Write a short bio about yourself

- **Travel Preferences:**
  - Traveler Type (Solo, Family, Friends, Couple, Business)
  - Travel Interests (Beaches, Mountains, Adventure, etc.)
  - Preferred Destinations (Choose from 50+ destinations)
  - Travel Style (Budget, Comfort, Luxury, etc.)
  - Preferred Season (Spring, Summer, etc.)

- **Social Media:**
  - Instagram handle

- **Profile Photo:**
  - Click on your current photo to upload a new one
  - Max size: 5MB

4. **Save Your Changes**
   - Click "💾 Save Changes" button
   - Or click "Cancel" to discard changes

## 🔔 What Happens After You Send a Request?

### Join Request Timeline:
```
1. You click "Join Trip" → Button becomes "⏳ Request Pending"
   ↓
2. Trip creator reviews your profile
   ↓
3a. If ACCEPTED → Button becomes "✅ Active - Open Chat"
    - You can now access the trip chat
    - You're officially part of the trip!

3b. If REJECTED → Request is removed
    - Button goes back to "Join Trip"
    - You can't send another request for this trip
```

### Booking Request Timeline:
```
1. You click "Book Seat" → Button becomes "⏳ Request Pending"
   ↓
2. Driver reviews your booking request
   ↓
3a. If ACCEPTED → Button becomes "✅ Active - View Details"
    - Your seat is confirmed
    - You can view ride details

3b. If REJECTED → Booking is removed
    - Button goes back to "Book Seat"
    - You can't send another booking for this ride
```

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: Why can't I send another request?
**A:** You can only send ONE request per trip/ride. This prevents spam and ensures fair access. If your request is rejected, you cannot request again.

### Q: How long does approval take?
**A:** It depends on the trip creator or driver. They will review your profile and decide. Be patient!

### Q: Can I cancel my pending request?
**A:** Currently, you need to wait for the creator/driver to respond. Future updates will add a cancel option.

### Q: What if I filled my profile wrong?
**A:** No problem! You can edit your profile anytime by going to Profile → Edit Profile.

### Q: How do I know if I'm accepted?
**A:** The button will change from yellow "⏳ Request Pending" to green "✅ Active". You'll also get an alert notification.

### Q: Can I join multiple trips?
**A:** Yes! You can join as many different trips as you want. The one-request rule only applies to each individual trip.
