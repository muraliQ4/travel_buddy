# Ride Share - Enhanced Features

## 🎉 New Features Added

## 📱 How to Use

### Offering a Ride:
1. Click **"🚙 Offer Ride"** tab
2. Fill in route, date, seats, and price
3. Add vehicle details and preferences
4. Click **"Post Your Ride"**
5. Manage from **"📋 My Offers"** tab

### Finding a Ride:
1. Click **"🔍 Find Rides"** tab
2. Browse available rides
3. Check ratings and reviews
4. Click **"Book Seat"** to reserve
5. Chat with driver if needed

### Managing Your Offers:
1. Go to **"📋 My Offers"** tab
2. View all active rides
3. Click **"💬 Chat"** to message passengers
4. Click **"📋 Manage"** to handle bookings
5. Monitor requests in real-time

### Using Group Chat:
1. Click **"💬 Group Chat"** tab
2. Select a ride from the list
3. Type your message
4. Press **Send** or hit Enter
5. View conversation history

### Rating a Driver:
1. Click **"⭐ Reviews"** on any ride
2. Click stars to set rating (1-5)
3. Write your review
4. Click **"Submit Review"**
